Shop Vegetables

Cabbage Varieties: All

View:

F1 Sunta

A round cabbage noted for sweetness and its relatively thin leaves. Good for coleslaw.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-May
Harvest: Jun-Oct

F1 Mozart

Tolerates dry summers. Can be left to stand and picked when required.
Type: Summer
Sow: Feb-May
Harvest: Jul-Oct

Greyhound

A traditional variety that produces a pointed heart. Compact size good for small beds.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-Jun
Harvest: Jul-Oct

F1 Summer Jewel

Spring Green. Loose headed variety with long picking season.
Type: Spring
Sow: Mar-Jun
Harvest: May-Nov

F1 Dutchman

Produces a pointed heart, good reputation for flavour.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-May
Harvest: Jun-Oct

Golden Acre Primo

A round headed variety, good for shredding in salads.
Type: Summer
Sow: Jan-Jun
Harvest: Jul-Oct

Red Drumhead

A beautiful red and white fleshed round cabbage with green blue outer leaves.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-May
Harvest: Aug-Oct

Wheelers Imperial

Ready for harvest in spring and autumn.
Type: Spring
Sow: Mar-May or Jul-Aug
Harvest: Apr-May or Sep-Nov

F1 Winterjewel

An extremely hardy variety with dark leaves. If left to mature, it forms a small pointed head.
Type: Spring
Sow: Jul-Aug
Harvest: Feb-Apr

Hilton

A Chinese cabbage with a barrel shaped head. Good for stir fry.
Type: Summer
Sow: Jun-Jul
Harvest: Aug-Oct

F1 Natsuki

Chinese cabbage. Pick young for use in stir fry, or let it develop to full size.
Type: Summer
Sow: May-Aug
Harvest: Jun-Oct

F1 Lodero

Good disease resistance. Large red cabbage.
Type: Summer
Sow: Feb-May
Harvest: Jun-Oct

F1 Sweetie

A collard good for 'pick and come again' harvesting throughout the summer.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-Jul
Harvest: Jun-Feb

F1 Kilazol

A large round headed cabbage harvested in autumn or winter.
Type: Winter
Sow: Feb-May
Harvest: Sep-Dec

Cabbice F1

Lettuce like leaves, thin and tender, ideal for salads.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-Jun
Harvest: Aug-Oct

January King 3

A big, colourful, and hardy cabbage. Leave on the plot over winter and harvest when required.
Type: Winter
Sow: Apr-May
Harvest: Dec-Mar

F1 Serpentine

A highly regarded summer cabbage for flavour and ease of growing.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-Apr
Harvest: Jul-Sep

Ormskirk Rearguard

An ideal winter cabbage. A Savoy type with big curly outer leaves and a round head.
Type: Winter
Sow: Apr-May
Harvest: Dec-Mar

Chateaurenard

A fast maturing variety that forms a pointed head.
Type: Summer
Sow: Apr-Jun
Harvest: Aug-Oct

Drago F1

A small round variety that provides an autumn harvest.
Type: Summer
Sow: May-Jun
Harvest: Sep-Oct

Vertus

Crinkled leaved variety able to stand overwinter until required.
Type: Winter
Sow: Apr-May
Harvest: Dec-Feb

Caraflex F1

Large pointed heads that stand well whilst waiting for harvesting.
Type: Summer
Sow: Jan-Jun or Oct
Harvest: Jun-Sep
