Cabbage Varieties: Summer

View:

F1 Sunta

A round cabbage noted for sweetness and its relatively thin leaves. Good for coleslaw.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-May
Harvest: Jun-Oct

F1 Mozart

Tolerates dry summers. Can be left to stand and picked when required.
Type: Summer
Sow: Feb-May
Harvest: Jul-Oct

Greyhound

A traditional variety that produces a pointed heart. Compact size good for small beds.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-Jun
Harvest: Jul-Oct

F1 Dutchman

Produces a pointed heart, good reputation for flavour.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-May
Harvest: Jun-Oct

Golden Acre Primo

A round headed variety, good for shredding in salads.
Type: Summer
Sow: Jan-Jun
Harvest: Jul-Oct

Red Drumhead

A beautiful red and white fleshed round cabbage with green blue outer leaves.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-May
Harvest: Aug-Oct

Hilton

A Chinese cabbage with a barrel shaped head. Good for stir fry.
Type: Summer
Sow: Jun-Jul
Harvest: Aug-Oct

F1 Natsuki

Chinese cabbage. Pick young for use in stir fry, or let it develop to full size.
Type: Summer
Sow: May-Aug
Harvest: Jun-Oct

F1 Lodero

Good disease resistance. Large red cabbage.
Type: Summer
Sow: Feb-May
Harvest: Jun-Oct

F1 Sweetie

A collard good for 'pick and come again' harvesting throughout the summer.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-Jul
Harvest: Jun-Feb

Cabbice F1

Lettuce like leaves, thin and tender, ideal for salads.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-Jun
Harvest: Aug-Oct

F1 Serpentine

A highly regarded summer cabbage for flavour and ease of growing.
Type: Summer
Sow: Mar-Apr
Harvest: Jul-Sep

Chateaurenard

A fast maturing variety that forms a pointed head.
Type: Summer
Sow: Apr-Jun
Harvest: Aug-Oct

Drago F1

A small round variety that provides an autumn harvest.
Type: Summer
Sow: May-Jun
Harvest: Sep-Oct

Caraflex F1

Large pointed heads that stand well whilst waiting for harvesting.
Type: Summer
Sow: Jan-Jun or Oct
Harvest: Jun-Sep
