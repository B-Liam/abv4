Lettuce Varieties: Butterhead

View:

Unrivalled

Green leaf medium sized lettuce.
Type: Loose Leaf
Sow: Mar-Jul
Harvest: May-Oct

John

Can be grown through the winter under protection.
Type: Butterhead
Sow: Sep-Feb
Harvest: Nov-May

All The Year Round

Can be grown all year round, but in winter will need protecting.
Type: Butterhead
Sow: Mar-Aug
Harvest: Apr-Oct

Winter Density

A dwarf variety often sown in the autumn.
Type: Butterhead
Sow: Mar-Sep
Harvest: Apr-Oct

Tom Thumb

A small fast growing lettuce that can be enjoyed throughout the summer months.
Type: Butterhead
Sow: Mar-Aug
Harvest: Jun-Oct

May King

Can be grown in a polytunnel or greenhouse overwinter.
Type: Butterhead
Sow: Aug-Sep
Harvest: Apr-May

Relay

Type of batavia lettuce with good flavour and texture. Drought resistant.
Type: Butterhead
Sow: Mar-Aug
Harvest: May-Oct
