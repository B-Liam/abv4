November Harvesting Guide

  • A month to start picking fresh hardy winter vegetables like Brussels sprouts, cabbage, kale, swedes, and leeks
  • Hardy varieties of pak choi, radishes, and winter spinach can add variety to an early winter harvest
  • Continue eating from any stores of potatoes, squash, carrots, beetroot, onions, and garlic
  • Continue picking and storing late varieties of apples
