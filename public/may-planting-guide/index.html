Planning May Planting

May Planting Guide

  • Now is the best time to plant frost sensitive summer crops like sweetcorn, French beans, and runner beans
  • Seed can be sown directly in the soil for many types of plant
  • Beware of late frosts that can damage plants that are not hardy (eg squash, tomatoes, cucumbers)
  • A good month to sow fast growing annual herbs like basil, parsley, and coriander
